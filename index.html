Quang Ninh Province

Quang Ninh is a province in northeastern Vietnam, known for its stunning natural landscapes, including Ha Long Bay, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

  • Location: Northeastern Vietnam
  • Capital: Ha Long City
  • Famous for: Ha Long Bay, Bai Tu Long Bay, and Cat Ba Island
  • Economy: Tourism, coal mining, and aquaculture
  • Culture: Rich in traditions and festivals
